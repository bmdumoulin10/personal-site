MyPantry

MyPantry is a recipe search web application that searches through over 1,000,000 recipes based on the ingredients you currently have at home. After logging in and saving ingredients to your online pantry, just click the "search my pantry" button to narrow your search to recipes you can already make. Click the image to be redirected to the site to try it out!
